About Us

A LOVE FOR OUR STYLE

Our specialty is nail art – beautiful looks that are elegant enough to be worn to a meeting, and stylish enough to be worn out to dinner. The Classic Collection features timeless designs that will never go out of style, while our Seasonal Collection changes twice a year to feature fresh new looks that lead manicure trends.

HEALTH & HYGIENE

The health of our customers and hygiene is our utmost priority. That’s why all our tools are either single-use (and recycled) or sterilized in a hospital-grade autoclave machine between each use. We also believe in ‘better-for-you’ products – all our polishes and gels are at least 3-free so you don’t need to worry about toxic chemicals.
